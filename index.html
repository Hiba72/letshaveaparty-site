About Let's have a party

"Let’s Have a Party" started from my love for creating unique and personalized sweet treats for my girls’ birthdays. After struggling to find the perfect party bags and favors, I began making my own — colorful cones and boxes with their favorite characters. The sweet compliments and frequent questions about where they were from inspired me to turn this hobby into a business. Now, I pour love and creativity into every order, making sure every celebration feels extra special.

Sweet cubes

These adorable and colorful sweet cubes are little boxes of joy, filled with an irresistible assortment of mouthwatering jelly treats. Each cube is packed with vibrant, soft, and chewy candies bursting with delightful flavors. Made with care and the finest ingredients — including sugar, glucose syrup, water, and modified starch derived from gluten-free wheat, maize, and potato — these jellies are a perfect combination of sweetness and texture. The gentle tang comes from a mix of natural acids like malic acid, sodium malate, lactic acid, and citric acid, balanced perfectly with natural flavorings and a pop of beautiful colors. These sweet cubes aren’t just a treat for your taste buds — they’re a feast for your eyes, making every bite as joyful as the one before!

Sweet cubes

These delightful and vibrant sweet cubes are a true celebration of color and flavor, each one packed with an irresistible selection of soft, chewy jellies. Bursting with a perfect balance of sweetness and tang, these treats are crafted from high-quality ingredients to ensure a delicious experience in every bite. They’re made with sugar, glucose syrup, and water, combined with modified starch from gluten-free sources like wheat, maize, and potato. The lively, zesty taste is enhanced by a thoughtful blend of acids — including malic acid, sodium malate, lactic acid, and citric acid — while natural flavorings and a rainbow of colors make them as beautiful as they are tasty. These sweet cubes promise a joyful, flavorful adventure that’s hard to resist!

Packaging according to your need

Our sweet and colorful jellies are not only a treat for the taste buds but also a feast for the eyes, beautifully packaged to match any special occasion. Whether it’s a fun-filled school event, a joyful Christmas celebration, the festive spirit of Eid, or the warmth of Easter, we create custom packaging tailored to your vision. From creative pencil-shaped boxes to elegant, themed designs, every package reflects thoughtfulness and care. We listen closely to our customers’ needs, making sure each box brings a smile even before it’s opened. With vibrant colors, delicious flavors, and personalized touch, our jelly packs turn every celebration into a sweeter, more memorable experience!
